Live-in Care

Live in care has grown rapidly in popularity because most people want to live out the sunset years of their lives in the comfort of their own home where there is familiarity and a world of memories with family.

An undeniable fact is that with a Live-in carer, a loved one can get individualised care, carefully personalised to suit their needs. In a care home, no matter how great it is, a loved one does not see the same carer all the time and carers can be overwhelmed with many residents needing attention at the same time such that, unfortunately, some loved ones fall through the cracks.

At home, your loved one has all the attention of the Live-in carer, they can come and go as they please, which is not possible in a care setting. Receiving person-centred care in your home is ideal, a carer there 24hours a day, helping you or your loved one remain independent, maintaining ties to the community, carrying on with your hobbies without restriction.

The client or their loved one is always in control of the way the care is delivered. All our Live-in carers are highly capable of using their initiative, multi tasking and know how to deal with unexpected situations.

In most cases, Live-in care is needed when a parent or loved one starts being forgetful, whether its to turn off the gas or water, or becomes a danger to themselves, or doing simple things is no longer simple.
